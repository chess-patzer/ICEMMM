About the Conference

The scope of the conference is to provide a platform for academicians, researchers and scientists, students to present their ongoing research work in a cutting edge and currently relevant area of materials, metallurgy and manufacturing. The advent of the digital age is seeing the robust growth of research in smart materials and technologies while the manufacturing processes ensures in making these new age products and prototypes. The need for energy savings, environment protection, green rated processes are further accelerators in the right direction. It is the endeavour of this conference to bring to the forefront the research works in Engineering materials, metallurgy and manufacturing by eminent scholars worldwide and share their success stories with an earnest audience.




Objectives

The principal objective of ICEMMM2018 is to provide a neutral platform for researchers, engineers, academicians as well as industrial professionals from all over the world to present their research results and development activities in Materials, Metallurgy and Manufacturing Technology. This conference provides opportunities for the delegates to exchange new ideas and application experiences face to face, to establish business or research relations and to find global partners for future collaboration. Submitted conference papers will be reviewed by technical committees of the Conference.
